## Do I need to know crypto to use CeyPay?

No. Customers pay in crypto, but you receive rupees directly. No crypto handling, no learning curve.

## Is CeyPay legal to use in Sri Lanka?

Yes. You receive LKR through licensed exchange partners. No claims of legal tender and no regulatory friction.

## How fast do I get my settlement?

Same-day within 24 hours or next-day depending on your payout schedule.

## What fees do merchants pay?

CeyPay offers zero setup fees and a simple, low fee per transaction. No hidden charges.

## What currencies can customers pay with?

They can pay using supported crypto assets via our exchange partners. You only receive rupees.

## Will this complicate my accounting?

No. You receive LKR and record it like a normal sale. Your accountant can follow regular bookkeeping.

## How hard is it to get started?

Less than 5 minutes to apply. Once approved, we set up your POS or QR and you can start accepting payments immediately.

You get local support via the CeylonCash team, backed by a community of 1,000+ active members. Our team is always available to help with onboarding, settlements, and transaction-related questions. ## Do I need any special devices?

No. You can accept payments using a QR code, payment link, or a free POS device if eligible.

## Can I offer refunds?

Crypto payments are instant and final. Once a payment is completed on-chain, it cannot be reversed or double-spent. This means there are virtually no chargebacks, with over 99.9% of transactions being final. If a merchant chooses to issue a refund, it can be handled manually off-chain or through settlement adjustments, based on mutual agreement with the customer. This model significantly reduces fraud, disputes, and chargeback risk compared to traditional card payments.

## Can online stores use CeyPay?

Yes. At the moment, CeyPay supports WooCommerce-based online stores. Merchants can get started quickly using our WooCommerce plugin, with other integrations planned for the future.
